Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

VitaMindGo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how VitaMindGo collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our iOS application (the "App").

By downloading or using the VitaMindGo App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health Data

VitaMindGo integrates with Apple HealthKit to collect and process the following health data:

  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Sleep analysis data (total sleep, deep sleep, REM sleep)
  • Step count and physical activity data
  • Exercise minutes and workout data
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • Body temperature measurements
  • Stand hours and movement data

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect non-personal information about how you interact with the App, including:

  • Features accessed and usage patterns
  • Device type and iOS version
  • App crashes and performance data
  • Anonymous usage analytics

2.3 AI Conversation Data

When you interact with the VitaMindGo AI assistant, the conversation content is processed to provide AI-powered health insights. These conversations may be processed using AI service providers (including but not limited to MiniMax, OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, or xAI) to deliver AI-powered health coaching and recommendations.

🔒 Privacy First: All health data collected through VitaMindGo stays on your device. We use on-device AI processing and/or trusted AI service providers for sensitive health data processing.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized health insights and recommendations
  • Sync health data with Apple Watch and HealthKit
  • Track your habits and send smart reminders
  • Generate health reports and trend analysis
  • Improve our App's features and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

4. Data Storage and Security

Your health data is stored securely on your iOS device using Apple's secure enclave. VitaMindGo implements the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions
  • On-device AI processing (CoreML) for sensitive health data
  • Secure data storage using iOS Keychain and HealthKit encryption
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability patches

5. Data Sharing

VitaMindGo does not sell, trade, or rent your personal health information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research or analytics purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify you.

We may share your information with:

  • Apple (for HealthKit integration and CloudKit sync)
  • AI service providers (for AI-powered features) - bound by strict data processing agreements
  • Service providers who assist in operating our App
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Apple HealthKit Permissions

VitaMindGo requests access to Apple HealthKit for the following purposes:

  • Read: Heart rate, sleep, steps, workouts, and related health metrics
  • Write: Habit completion data and health insights
  • Background Delivery: Real-time sync of health data changes

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through iOS Settings > Privacy > HealthKit.

7. Third-Party AI Services

VitaMindGo may use the following third-party AI service providers to power your AI health assistant experience:

  • MiniMax - Primary AI service provider
  • OpenAI - Alternative AI service option
  • Anthropic - Alternative AI service option
  • Google Gemini - Alternative AI service option
  • DeepSeek - Alternative AI service option
  • xAI (Grok) - Alternative AI service option

All AI service providers are bound by strict data processing agreements that prohibit them from using your conversation data for training purposes or retaining it beyond the session duration.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal health data stored in VitaMindGo
  • Export your data in machine-readable format (PDF, CSV)
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Control notification preferences
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions at any time
  • Choose which AI service provider to use for your conversations

🗑️ Data Deletion: To delete your VitaMindGo account and all data, go to Settings > Privacy > VitaMindGo > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers and devices.

9. Children's Privacy

VitaMindGo is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your continued use of VitaMindGo after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
