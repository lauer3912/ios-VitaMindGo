## Overview

VitaMind is an AI-powered health assistant that combines intelligent health monitoring, habit tracking, and personalized wellness recommendations. The app integrates deeply with Apple Watch and HealthKit to provide comprehensive health insights powered by on-device AI.

## Technical Implementation Notes

- **Minimum iOS Version:** 16.0
- **Supported Devices:** iPhone, iPad
- **Apple Watch:** watchOS 9.0+ (optional but recommended)
- **Frameworks:** SwiftUI, HealthKit, WatchConnectivity, CoreML, WidgetKit
- **Data Storage:** CloudKit for sync, CoreData for local cache
- **AI Engine:** On-device CoreML models + MiniMax API for advanced queries
