# VitaMindGo

Your AI-Powered Health Companion — track heart rate, sleep, habits, and get personalized AI wellness insights.

## Why VitaMindGo?

VitaMindGo is a privacy-first iOS app that combines AI with your health data to help you build better habits, sleep better, and understand your body better.

## Features

- **Real-time Heart Rate Monitoring** — Apple Watch + iPhone integration
- **AI Health Coach** — On-device, private AI that learns your patterns
- **Habit Tracker** — Daily check-ins, streaks, and achievements
- **Sleep Analysis** — Trends, insights, and recommendations
- **Apple Watch App** — Glanceable metrics on your wrist

## Privacy

All AI processing happens **on-device**. Your health data never leaves your iPhone. We don't have servers storing your data.
